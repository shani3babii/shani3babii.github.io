Who Are We...

N Y A C

NOT. YOUR. AVERAGE. CANDLE.



With a love...more like an obsession for candles and a desire to use natural ingredients to make a unique candle. NYAC was born. A candle company based in New York with one goal only to "INDULGE YOUR SENSE". Instead of an ordinary candle that attacks one of your senses we brought a FRESH new twist. Something new! Imagine a candle that also acts as massage oil, 2 in 1. From the alluring aroma of our candles. To the warm sensation felt at the first drop of oil unto the skin. It’s truly sensational and comforting. We want you to feel comfortable and relaxed.

As you welcome us into your space we want to add to the ambience. No matter your taste we are here to set the tone for you. We have a wide selection of candles. We are the gift that keeps on giving as we are constantly adding to our selection of aromas and accessories to explore your desires and wants. From the stresses of the world escape to a space that allows us to do all the work for you do you can feel at peace.
